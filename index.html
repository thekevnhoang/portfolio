The Inspiration Behind this Webpage

Welcome to my portfolio page, a personal website where you will find many of my projects, ideas, and plans! I'm a 22 year old Computer Science undergraduate with a lot of passion for arts, dancing, music, and of course programming. My reason for starting this page is not only to showcase my programs and projects, but to also provide an outlet for my hobbies and other artists alike too. My dream is to develop a social media webpage where musicians, dancers, and all artists alike can share their work and network with one another. Aspiring music artists can share their music and dancers can choreograph to those created songs. So my plan is like a MySpace page to some extent, just with the intention of promoting individuality and creativity. I'm totally excited to share what I have in store for you all.
