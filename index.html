The Inspiration Behind this Webpage

Welcome to my portfolio page, a personal website where you will find many of my projects, ideas, and plans! I'm a 22 year old Computer Science undergraduate with a lot of passion for arts, dancing, music, and of course programming. My reason for starting this page is not only to showcase my programs and projects, but to also provide an outlet for my hobbies and other artists alike too. My dream is to develop a social media webpage where musicians, dancers, and all artists alike can share their work and network with one another. Aspiring music artists can share their music and dancers can choreograph to those created songs. So my plan is like a MySpace page to some extent, just with the intention of promoting individuality and creativity. I'm totally excited to share what I have in store for you all. Down below is a video of one of my latest works, thanks for giving my page a look and stay tuned for future updates! Follow my Instagram to see more videos!

Super Chess Bros Launch!

I'm excited to announce that my next project Super Chess Bros. is about to release very soon. This game will feature a game of chess, but with many of the famous video game characters that you all know and love. Even though it may sound like a boring game of chess, it's actually a little more advanced than that. For this game of 2, players will be able to create their own team from a wide assortment of famous characters, all with unique moving patterns making every game a unique experience! The inspiration of this application comes from my love for video games and also my pretty high skill level in chess. When released, the game will be hosted on a web browser for players to compete and test their might on their own devices. Stay tuned to see the release!
